Investing

1. Why invest

To get rich. More precisely: to compound real purchasing power over long periods.

If you are 10% per year ahead of inflation, you double your real wealth in about 7.27 years.

If you are 20% per year ahead of inflation, you double your real wealth in about 3.80 years.

2. Learning materials for complete beginners

Foundations

  • Very first hook: short Buffett clips
  • Buffett on temperament > IQ
  • Buffett on Mr. Market
  • Buffett on circle of competence
  • The Little Book That Still Beats the Market — Joel Greenblatt
  • One Up On Wall Street — Peter Lynch
  • The Essays of Warren Buffett — edited by Lawrence Cunningham

Then

  • Selected Berkshire Hathaway shareholder letters
  • Basic accounting: income statement, balance sheet, cash flow statement
  • Annual reports of simple businesses you already understand

Avoid at the beginning

  • Trading videos
  • Technical analysis
  • Macro doom content
  • Complex valuation models
  • Stock tips
